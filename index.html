Welcome to Peak Rental Services!


Here at Peak Rental Services, we're 100% New Zealand! We started as a small service started by former university students Wei and Tory who decided that the Wellington public needed more bikes for rent. They started with only a few bikes but slowly grew to have more and more thanks to the expertise of Tory, who is an e-commerce expert. Wei made sure that the bikes they were getting were of good quality as he himself is a mountain bike enthusiast.
